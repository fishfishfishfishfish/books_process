柯林斯英汉双解大词典

conceal /kənˈsiːl/ CET4 TEM4 ( concealing, concealed, conceals )

  • 1.

    V-T If you conceal something, you cover it or hide it carefully. 掩盖; 隐藏

    例：

    Frances decided to conceal the machine behind a hinged panel.

    弗朗西丝决定把那台机器藏到一块可开合的镶板后面。

  • 2.

    V-T If you conceal a piece of information or a feeling, you do not let other people know about it. 隐瞒 (信息); 掩饰 (情感)

    例：

    Robert could not conceal his relief.

    罗伯特掩饰不住自己如释重负的心情。

  • 3.

    V-T If something conceals something else, it covers it and prevents it from being seen. 遮盖

    例：

    ...a pair of carved Indian doors which conceal a built-in cupboard.

    …一对遮挡嵌入壁橱的印度式雕花门。

词根： conceal

n.

concealment 隐藏，隐蔽；隐匿处

concealer 遮瑕膏/遮瑕霜

screen, hide, conceal

这组词都有“隐藏，隐蔽”的意思，其区别是：

screen 指把处于被发现的人或物掩盖起来，使其不被发现。

hide 普通用词，既可指有意地隐藏，又可指无意或偶然地藏匿。

conceal 正式用词，多强调有目的、有意识，巧妙地进行隐藏或隐瞒。
